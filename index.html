About Our Mission

The High Desert Memory Recreation Center is a community-based program in development, created to provide a safe, dignified, and joyful space for individuals living with memory challenges. Our goal is to reduce caregiver stress and strengthen community support while offering purposeful and creative engagement for every participant.

Why This Matters

In recent years, the Morongo Basin has seen a rise in Silver Alerts and cases of senior wandering. Through structured social programs, community partnerships, and memory-specific recreation, our mission is to enhance safety, connection, and quality of life for our seniors while giving families peace of mind.
